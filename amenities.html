Our Amenities

24/7 CCTV Security

At UJ PALLAZZIO, your safety is our utmost priority. Our state-of-the-art CCTV system provides round-the-clock surveillance, ensuring a secure environment throughout your stay. Our security team monitors all areas of the hotel, including entrances, exits, corridors, and common areas, providing you with peace of mind and allowing you to fully relax and enjoy your time with us. Whether you're here for business or leisure, rest assured that you're protected by our comprehensive security measures.

Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel. Whether you need to catch up on work emails, video call with loved ones, or stream your favorite shows, our reliable internet access ensures you're always connected. With coverage extending from your room to all common areas, including our lobby, restaurant, and outdoor spaces, you can enjoy seamless connectivity no matter where you are in the hotel. Our IT support team is always on hand to assist with any connectivity issues, ensuring your online experience is as smooth as possible.

Free Parking

Enjoy the convenience of our spacious, complimentary parking facility. Whether you're arriving in your own vehicle or renting one during your stay, our secure parking area provides peace of mind and easy access to your car whenever you need it. The well-lit parking lot is monitored 24/7 by our security team, ensuring the safety of your vehicle. For guests with electric vehicles, we also offer charging stations. Our valet parking service is available upon request, allowing you to step out of your car and into our welcoming lobby with ease.

24-Hour Front Desk

Our friendly and professional staff is at your service around the clock. Whether you're checking in late at night, need early morning assistance, or require help at any hour, our 24-hour front desk ensures that your needs are met promptly and efficiently. Our multilingual team is well-versed in local attractions, dining options, and transportation services, ready to provide recommendations and assist with bookings. From wake-up calls to arranging airport transfers, handling special requests, or addressing any concerns, we're committed to making your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible, any time of day or night.

Luggage Storage

Travel light and worry-free with our secure luggage storage service. Whether you've arrived early before check-in or have a late departure after check-out, we offer a safe place to store your belongings. Our dedicated storage area is monitored and accessible only by authorized staff, ensuring the security of your items. This service allows you to explore the city or attend meetings without the burden of heavy bags. For valuable items, we provide safety deposit boxes for extra peace of mind. Our staff handles your luggage with care, and we can assist with transporting your bags to and from your room upon request.

Daily Housekeeping

Experience pristine cleanliness with our meticulous daily housekeeping service. Our dedicated team of housekeeping professionals ensures that your room remains fresh, tidy, and comfortable throughout your stay. We use eco-friendly cleaning products and follow strict hygiene protocols to maintain a healthy environment. From making your bed with crisp, clean linens to restocking toiletries and refreshing towels, we pay attention to every detail. If you have specific preferences or requirements, such as hypoallergenic bedding or additional amenities, our housekeeping staff is happy to accommodate your needs. For extended stays, we offer deep cleaning services and can arrange for laundry and dry cleaning upon request.

Elevator

Enjoy easy access to all floors with our modern, well-maintained elevators. We prioritize the comfort and convenience of all our guests, ensuring smooth vertical transportation throughout the hotel. Our elevators are equipped with the latest safety features and undergo regular maintenance checks to guarantee reliable operation. They are spacious enough to accommodate guests with luggage or mobility aids comfortably. For guests who prefer, we also offer stair access. In case of any unforeseen issues, our maintenance team is on call 24/7 to swiftly resolve any elevator-related concerns, ensuring your stay is uninterrupted and comfortable.

Dry Cleaning

Keep your clothes fresh and crisp with our professional dry cleaning service. We understand the importance of looking your best, whether you're here for business or leisure. Our partnership with a trusted local dry cleaner ensures high-quality care for your garments. We offer quick turnaround times, typically within 24 hours, to accommodate your schedule. From suits and dresses to delicate fabrics, our service handles a wide range of clothing items with expertise. For your convenience, we provide laundry bags and itemized forms in your room. Simply fill out the form, place your items in the bag, and our staff will handle the rest, returning your clothes clean, pressed, and ready to wear.

Safety Deposit Boxes

Secure your valuables with peace of mind using our safety deposit boxes. Located at the front desk, these boxes are available to all guests free of charge during their stay. Each box is equipped with a unique key or electronic code, ensuring that only you have access to your belongings. Our safety deposit boxes are suitable for storing a variety of items, including passports, jewelry, important documents, and electronic devices. The area housing the safety deposit boxes is under constant surveillance and accessible only by authorized personnel. For items requiring extra security or larger storage space, please speak to our front desk staff, who can arrange additional secure storage options tailored to your needs.

Friendly Staff

At UJ PALLAZZIO, our warm and friendly staff is the heart of our hospitality. Our team is carefully selected and trained to provide exceptional service with a personal touch. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted with genuine smiles and a willingness to go above and beyond to make your stay memorable. Our multilingual staff is knowledgeable about local attractions, dining options, and cultural experiences, ready to offer personalized recommendations tailored to your interests. Whether you need assistance with room service, require information about hotel facilities, or seek advice on exploring the area, our attentive team is always eager to help. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels valued and cared for throughout their stay.

Smoking Area

While we maintain a smoke-free environment inside the hotel to ensure the comfort of all guests, we understand that some of our visitors may smoke. To accommodate their needs, we have designated a comfortable outdoor smoking area. This specially designed space is conveniently located and easily accessible from the hotel lobby. The area is well-ventilated, sheltered from the elements, and equipped with comfortable seating and proper ash disposal facilities. We've taken care to create an environment that is pleasant for smokers while minimizing the impact on non-smoking guests. The smoking area is regularly cleaned and maintained to ensure a tidy appearance. We kindly ask all guests to use this designated area for smoking and to refrain from smoking in rooms or other public spaces within the hotel.
